Build your transition plan

Answer a few questions and the app will build a custom game plan.

This tool gives practical guidance, not legal advice or an official VA determination. Always confirm benefits and deadlines through official VA, DoD, and state channels.

What this app covers

This is built to be simple, useful, and straight to the point.

1. Timeline-based to do list

Different tasks matter at 12 months out than they do at 30 days out. This app changes based on your timeline.

2. Job ideas that match what you actually did

Your MOS, rank, and years in service are translated into civilian roles, leadership level, and keyword ideas for your resume.

3. VA and benefit steps in plain English

GI Bill, disability claim timing, health care, home loan, education comparison tools, and where to start.

4. State and national support

You will get direct links, simple explanations, and major veteran organizations worth knowing about after you get out.

Stuff people miss: copies of records, personal email changes, LinkedIn, civilian clothes for interviews, local DMV rules, unemployment eligibility, childcare plans, med refills, and actually booking appointments early instead of assuming there is time.
